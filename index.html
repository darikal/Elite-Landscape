About Us

Elite Landscape and Irrigation is a family owned and operated company proudly serving Pasco and Pinellas county. Our mission? Deliver dependable service, consistent quality, and landscapes our customers can be proud of. Despite our age of technology, we still strongly believe “word of mouth” is how a successful company continues to thrive; thus leaving you with a positive experience is and will always be our number one priority. What we believe sets us apart is that we understand your property, be it commercial or residential, is our “billboard” at the end of the day. We want the same thing as you, Elite results. Most of our customers come from referrals, which tells us we are doing something right.


With decades of hands on experience in irrigation and landscape care, our team understands that every property is unique. From irrigation needs to routine landscape maintenance and design, we focus on doing the job right the first time and treating every property as our own, or again, a billboard for our work.


Say goodbye to corporate speak that makes you feel good in the moment but doesn’t actually deliver the results you asked for.


As a family business, we value relationships just as much as results. What does that really mean? With Elite, you’re not dealing with layers of management leaving you with a high risk of getting lost in the shuffle. Say good bye to waiting weeks for a response and action taken. When you call or message us, you’re speaking directly with people who run the company. That means clear answers, quick responses, and no getting passed around between departments.
